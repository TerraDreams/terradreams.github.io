What inspired and motivates you to do art?

My own incapabilities and that I may Regret not doing things now, and always looking for another opportunity next time.

I don't like the fact that I don't know how to execute an idea that I have in mind whenever I need it, and that's enough for me to keep me motivated at learning new things everyday. I believe being adaptable is the key to life and satisfaction.

I started drawing at the early age of 5 years old, as soon as I was conscious about the world around me.

Anime is one of the greatest factor as to why and what my niche is in drawing today (excluding the fact that I can of course adjust my style based on what I need whether it be semi-realism or realism).

My greatest influence would be my oldest sister who introduced anime to me and that was when I started figuring out what and what I did not like in art.
